The bin that
talks to your app.

Phase 2 integrates RFID and optical sensors that sync directly with the Outfitted app — closing the loop between your physical wardrobe and your digital stylist.

📡
Auto Wear Tracking
RFID sensors detect which items enter the bin and automatically log them as worn in the Outfitted app. Your closet score updates without lifting a finger.

🔄
Resale Triggers
Items that sit in "Rewear" for 30+ days without being worn again automatically surface as resale candidates in the app. Earn from what you're not wearing.
