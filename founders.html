Johnathan Joseph
Founder & CEO

Johnathan Joseph is the founder and CEO of Outfitted AI LLC. He built Outfitted from zero to a functioning iOS app in under six months using an AI-leveraged development model — with autonomous agents handling operations while he operates at the CEO level.

Holding an MS in Entrepreneurship & Innovation from USC Marshall, JJ brings a background spanning corporate finance, technology process improvement, and AI systems design. His core thesis: the next generation of companies will be built by founders who operate as architects of AI workforces, not managers of human ones.

Outfitted placed 2nd at the USC Marshall HTA AI Product Challenge in April 2026.

Tiffany Brown
Co-Founder · Growth & Style

Tiffany Brown is the co-founder of Outfitted and the creative intelligence behind the platform's styling engine. A former professional fashion stylist with deep brand relationships across the industry, Tiffany brings the real-world expertise that makes Outfitted's AI recommendations feel human — not algorithmic.

Her professional styling career spans work with major fashion brands including Fabletics. She leads Outfitted's growth strategy, brand partnerships, and social media — and is the direct source of the styling intelligence that powers the AI outfit engine.

Tiffany's philosophy: great style is about confidence first, fashion second. That belief is encoded into every outfit suggestion Outfitted generates.
