How It Works

Simple integration,
real results.

Getting your brand onto Outfitted is straightforward. We handle the integration — you focus on conversions.

01
Apply to Partner
Reach out via email or through your affiliate network. We review all applications and respond within 3 business days with options tailored to your brand.
02
Get Integrated
Your products are added to our curated shop feed and styled into AI outfit suggestions. Affiliate links are tracked end-to-end with full attribution.

Every click and purchase is tracked and attributed. Commission-based model means our incentives are fully aligned with your sales outcomes.
