How It Works

Three steps to a
smarter wardrobe.

Outfitted connects your closet, your style, and your shopping — all in one place.

01
Build Your Closet
Upload your clothes using your camera or photo library. Our AI automatically categorizes each item, assigns style tags, and builds your personal wardrobe database.
02
Get Styled Daily
Tell us your occasion and your AI stylist selects the perfect outfit from your actual closet. Try it on virtually before you wear it.
03
Shop & Earn
Discover new pieces personalized to your style DNA. List items you no longer wear to our P2P marketplace and earn directly through the app. Your closet works for you.
Features

Everything your wardrobe
needs to perform.

Built with proprietary styling intelligence, AI virtual try-on, and a multi-model AI architecture.

✦
AI Outfit Suggestions
Powered by proprietary professional styling intelligence. Every suggestion considers your body type, occasion, color theory, and personal style profile — not just your categories.
👗
Virtual Try-On
See any garment on your actual body before you wear or buy it. AI composites your selfie with closet items or shop products in seconds.
🎤
Voice Styling
Tap the mic and describe what you need. Your AI stylist responds with a curated look. Hands-free, conversational, instant.
🛍️
Smart Shop Tab
Leading fashion retailers curated to your closet's style DNA. Not random products — pieces that actually complement what you already own.
💰
P2P Resale
List unworn items from your closet to the Outfitted marketplace in under a minute. Stripe-powered checkout. Your closet generates income.
📅
Weekly Outfit Planner
AI-generated 7-day plans with distinct archetypes per day. Tracks what you wear, scores your closet utilization, and surfaces resale candidates automatically.
