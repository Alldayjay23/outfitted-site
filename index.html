The Solution

Your closet.
Intelligently styled.

Outfitted uses AI to turn your existing wardrobe into a personal styling engine — with outfit suggestions, virtual try-on, and a marketplace to shop and earn, all in one place.

✦
AI Outfit Suggestions
GPT-powered styling engine selects outfits from your actual closet based on occasion, weather, and your personal style tags. Try Again for instant variety. Ask Stylist for conversational refinement.
👗
Virtual Try-On
Fashn.ai-powered try-on composites your photo with any garment — from your closet, the marketplace, or retailer feeds — before you wear or buy it.
🎤
Voice Styling
Tap the mic and describe what you need. OpenAI Whisper transcribes your request and the AI stylist responds with a curated look — hands-free, conversational, instant.
🛍️
Smart Shopping
Retailer tab surfaces ASOS and eBay products personalized to your closet's category profile. Affiliate revenue activates on approval. Skimlinks integration pending.
💰
Wear, Shop, Earn
List unworn items from your closet directly to the P2P marketplace. Outfitted takes 10–15% commission on sales. Closet score gamifies wardrobe utilization.
📅
Weekly Planner
AI-generated 7-day outfit plans that rotate styles, avoid repeats, and adapt to your schedule. Each day gets a distinct archetype — Streetwear, Elevated Casual, and more.

Why now?

AI styling models have reached consumer-ready quality — GPT-4o, Fashn.ai, and Whisper make real-time personalization viable at scale for the first time.
The resale market is projected to reach $350B by 2027 — Outfitted sits at the intersection of primary retail and secondhand, capturing both.
AI-leveraged development compresses the build timeline from years to months — Outfitted shipped v6 with a team of two and an AI workforce.
Post-pandemic consumer behavior shifted permanently toward value, sustainability, and digital-first shopping — Outfitted is built for this moment.
