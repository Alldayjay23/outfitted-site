The Solution

Your closet.
Intelligently styled.

Outfitted uses AI to turn your existing wardrobe into a personal styling engine — with outfit suggestions, virtual try-on, and a marketplace to shop and earn.

✦
AI Outfit Suggestions
GPT-4o powered by Tiffany Brown's professional styling rules. Body type, occasion, color theory, and personal style profile — all factored into every suggestion.
👗
Virtual Try-On
Fashn.ai composites your photo with any garment before you wear or buy it. See it on your actual body, not a model.
🛍️
Smart Shopping
ASOS and eBay products personalized to your closet's style DNA with affiliate tracking. More retailers being added via Sovrn and direct brand partnerships.
💰
P2P Resale
List unworn items directly to the Outfitted marketplace. Stripe Connect handles checkout. 12% platform commission on every sale.
🤖
AI Agent Workforce
6 autonomous agents handle operations — daily briefs, growth reports, engineering reviews. Zero ops headcount. The company runs itself.
📦
Physical Products
The Reset Bin — a premium smart wardrobe organizer — and branded Outfitted merch extend the platform from digital to physical. Samples in production.

Why now?

AI styling models have reached consumer-ready quality — GPT-4o, Fashn.ai, and Whisper make real-time personalization viable at scale for the first time.
The resale market is projected to reach $350B by 2027 — Outfitted sits at the intersection of primary retail and secondhand, capturing both.
AI-leveraged development compresses the build timeline from years to months — Outfitted shipped v6 with a team of two and an AI workforce.
Post-pandemic consumer behavior shifted toward value, sustainability, and digital-first shopping — Outfitted is built for this moment.
